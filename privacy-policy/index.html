Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this Discord music bot collects, uses, and protects information related to your use of the service.

2. Information Collected

The bot may collect the following information:

  • Guild ID (server ID) for configuration purposes
  • Channel or message IDs (only if necessary for queue management or playback)
  • No personal data such as usernames, emails, or messages are stored

3. How Information Is Used

Any data collected is used solely to operate core bot functions, such as playing music, maintaining queues, or customizing behavior per server.

4. Data Sharing

No data is sold or shared with third parties. All data remains internal to the bot's operation.

5. Data Storage

Minimal data is stored temporarily in memory or a database (if applicable) for active sessions. We do not persist user information long-term.

6. Your Rights

If you are a server owner and wish to remove data associated with your server, you can remove the bot from the server. This will trigger deletion of any associated data.

7. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact the developer via Discord or through the official support server.
